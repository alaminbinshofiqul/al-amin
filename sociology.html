About Our Department

Excellence in Sociological Education

The Department of Sociology at Rangpur Government College has been a beacon of academic excellence since its establishment. We are committed to providing comprehensive education in sociological theory, research methods, and practical applications.

Our department focuses on understanding social phenomena, human behavior, and societal structures. We prepare students to become critical thinkers, researchers, and change agents in society.

With state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty, we offer a dynamic learning environment that encourages intellectual growth and social awareness.

Academic Programs

Bachelor of Arts (Honors)

4-year comprehensive program covering all major areas of sociology including theory, research methods, and specialized fields.

Master of Arts

2-year advanced program focusing on specialized areas of sociology with emphasis on research and thesis work.

Research Programs

Opportunities for independent research projects and collaboration with faculty on ongoing research initiatives.

Community Outreach

Practical fieldwork and community engagement programs to apply sociological knowledge in real-world settings.

Research & Publications

Our department is actively engaged in cutting-edge research that addresses contemporary social issues and contributes to the advancement of sociological knowledge.

Rural Development Studies

Comprehensive research on rural communities, agricultural practices, and sustainable development in northern Bangladesh.

Gender & Society

In-depth analysis of gender roles, women's empowerment, and social equality in contemporary Bangladeshi society.

Urban Sociology

Studies on urbanization patterns, migration trends, and social dynamics in rapidly growing cities.

Education & Social Mobility

Research on educational access, social stratification, and opportunities for social advancement.
