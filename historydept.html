About Our Department

Excellence in Historical Education

The Department of History at Rangpur Government College has been a guardian of historical knowledge and cultural heritage since its establishment. We are dedicated to fostering critical thinking, research skills, and historical consciousness among our students.

Our comprehensive curriculum covers ancient civilizations, medieval periods, modern history, and contemporary global developments. We emphasize both theoretical understanding and practical research methodologies.

With our extensive library, archives, and experienced faculty, we provide students with the tools to become historians, researchers, educators, and informed citizens.

Historical Milestones:

1965: Department established with focus on South Asian history
1980: Expanded curriculum to include world history
2000: Digital archives and research center established
2020: International collaboration programs initiated

Academic Programs

Bachelor of Arts (Honors)

4-year comprehensive program covering ancient, medieval, modern, and contemporary history with emphasis on critical analysis.

Master of Arts

2-year advanced program focusing on specialized historical periods, research methodology, and thesis work.

Research Programs

Independent research opportunities in archival studies, oral history projects, and historical documentation.

Heritage Studies

Practical fieldwork in archaeological sites, museums, and cultural heritage preservation projects.

Research & Publications

Our department conducts extensive research in various fields of history, contributing to the preservation and understanding of our rich cultural heritage.

Islamic History

Comprehensive research on Islamic civilization, Mughal architecture, and the spread of Islam in the Indian subcontinent.

Liberation War Studies

Documentation and analysis of Bangladesh's independence movement, oral history collection, and freedom fighter interviews.

Colonial History

Studies on British colonial administration, economic policies, and their impact on Bengali society and culture.

Women in History

Research on women's roles in different historical periods, gender studies, and feminist historiography.
