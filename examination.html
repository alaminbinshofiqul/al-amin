Examination Guidelines

Important rules, regulations, and guidelines for all examination activities. Please read carefully before appearing for exams.

⚖️

Examination Rules

  • ✓ Arrive at least 30 minutes before exam time
  • ✓ Bring valid student ID and admit card
  • ✓ No electronic devices allowed in exam hall
  • ✓ Follow seating arrangements strictly
📝

Allowed Materials

  • ✓ Blue or black ink pens only
  • ✓ Pencils for diagrams and graphs
  • ✓ Scientific calculator (if permitted)
  • ✓ Geometric instruments for technical subjects
⚠️

Code of Conduct

  • ✓ Maintain complete silence during exam
  • ✓ No communication with other students
  • ✓ Raise hand for any assistance needed
  • ✓ Submit answer sheets before leaving
🏥

Health & Safety

  • ✓ Follow COVID-19 safety protocols
  • ✓ Inform invigilator if feeling unwell
  • ✓ Maintain proper hygiene standards
  • ✓ Emergency contact numbers available
