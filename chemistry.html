About Our Department

Excellence in Chemical Education

The Department of Chemistry at Rangpur Government College stands as a center of scientific excellence, fostering innovation and discovery in the molecular sciences. We inspire students to explore the fascinating world of atoms, molecules, and chemical reactions.

Our comprehensive curriculum covers organic chemistry, inorganic chemistry, physical chemistry, and analytical chemistry. We prepare students for careers in pharmaceuticals, materials science, environmental science, and research.

With modern laboratories, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art equipment, we provide hands-on experience in chemical synthesis, analysis, and research methodologies.

Academic Programs

Bachelor of Science (Honors)

4-year comprehensive program covering all major areas of chemistry with extensive laboratory work and research projects.

Master of Science

2-year advanced program focusing on specialized areas including organic, inorganic, physical, and analytical chemistry.

Industrial Chemistry Track

Specialized program combining chemistry with industrial applications, preparing students for careers in chemical industries.

Research Programs

Independent research opportunities in cutting-edge chemistry fields with access to modern analytical instruments.

Research & Innovation

Our department conducts innovative research in various fields of chemistry, contributing to scientific advancement and technological development.

Medicinal Chemistry

Development of new pharmaceutical compounds, drug design, and synthesis of bioactive molecules for therapeutic applications.

Green Chemistry

Environmentally friendly chemical processes, sustainable synthesis methods, and pollution prevention strategies.

Materials Science

Synthesis and characterization of advanced materials, polymers, and nanomaterials for technological applications.

Environmental Chemistry

Analysis of environmental pollutants, water treatment technologies, and development of eco-friendly solutions.
