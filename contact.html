Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about contacting and visiting our college.

What are the general office hours of the college?
The general office hours of Rangpur Government College are from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM, Sunday through Thursday. On Fridays, the office is open from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM, and it remains closed on Saturdays except for the Admissions Office which is open from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM.
How can I schedule a campus tour?
To schedule a campus tour, you can contact our Admissions Office at +880 1234 567891 or email admissions@rangpurcollege.edu.bd. Campus tours are typically available Monday through Thursday from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We recommend scheduling your tour at least one week in advance.
What is the best way to contact a specific department?
The best way to contact a specific department is to email the department directly using the email addresses provided on our website. You can also call the department's direct line during office hours. If you're unsure which department to contact, you can reach out to our main office at +880 1234 567890, and they will direct your inquiry to the appropriate department.
How long does it typically take to receive a response to an email inquiry?
We strive to respond to all email inquiries within 24-48 business hours. During peak periods such as admission season or the beginning of a new semester, response times may be slightly longer. If your inquiry is urgent, we recommend calling our main office directly.
Is there parking available for visitors on campus?
Yes, we have designated visitor parking areas on campus. Visitor parking is available near the main entrance and is clearly marked. Parking is free for the first two hours, after which a nominal fee applies. If you're visiting for an official appointment or event, please inform the relevant department in advance, and they can arrange for extended free parking.
How can I contact the college for emergency situations?
For emergency situations during office hours, please call our emergency hotline at +880 1234 567892. After hours, you can reach our campus security at +880 1234 567899. For medical emergencies on campus, contact our health center directly at +880 1234 567898. All emergency numbers are operational 24/7.
