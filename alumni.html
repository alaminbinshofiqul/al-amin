About Our Alumni Association

Established in 1980, the Rangpur Government College Alumni Association has been connecting graduates and fostering a community of excellence for over four decades.

Our Legacy of Excellence

The Rangpur Government College Alumni Association was established with the mission to foster a lifelong connection between the college and its graduates. We strive to create a vibrant community that supports both current students and alumni in their professional and personal endeavors.

Our association organizes regular events, mentorship programs, and networking opportunities to strengthen the bonds between generations of RGC graduates. We also contribute to the development of the college through scholarships, infrastructure improvements, and academic initiatives.

Alumni Voices

Hear what our alumni have to say about their experiences at Rangpur Government College and how it shaped their lives and careers.

My years at Rangpur Government College shaped who I am today. The values of integrity, excellence, and service that I learned here have guided me throughout my career in public service. The friendships I formed and the mentorship I received continue to be invaluable assets in my life.

Aminul Islam

Former Minister of Education, Class of 1982

CEO, GreenTech Solutions, Class of 1998
