About Our College

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1902

Rangpur Government College, established in 1902, is one of the oldest and most prestigious educational institutions in Bangladesh. With over a century of academic excellence, our college has produced countless leaders, scholars, and professionals who have contributed significantly to the nation's development.
