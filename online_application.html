Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our online application process.

What documents do I need to prepare for my application?
For most applications, you'll need to prepare digital copies of your academic transcripts, certificates, national ID or passport, recent photograph, and any program-specific documents. All documents should be clear, legible scans in PDF or JPG format.
How long does the application process take?
The online application form typically takes about 15-20 minutes to complete if you have all required documents ready. After submission, application review times vary by program: admission applications are typically processed within 2-3 weeks, scholarship applications within 4 weeks, and hostel applications within 1-2 weeks.
Can I save my application and complete it later?
Yes, our system automatically saves your progress. You can log out and return to complete your application at any time before the deadline. Simply log back into your account and click on "Continue Application" to resume where you left off.
How do I check the status of my application?
You can check your application status by logging into your account and visiting the "Application Status" section on your dashboard. You'll see real-time updates on your application, including whether it's under review, approved, or if additional information is needed.
What payment methods are accepted for application fees?
We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, mobile banking (bKash, Nagad, Rocket), and online banking transfers. All payments are processed through our secure payment gateway to ensure your financial information remains protected.
