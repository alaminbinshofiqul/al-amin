Rangpur Government College, established in 1902, stands as a beacon of academic excellence in northern Bangladesh.

With a rich history spanning over a century, our institution has consistently nurtured the brightest minds, producing graduates who have gone on to make significant contributions in various fields both nationally and internationally.

Our undergraduate programs blend traditional academic rigor with modern educational approaches, preparing students not just for examinations but for the challenges of a rapidly changing global landscape.

Our Facilities

Modern Libraries

Our central library houses over 100,000 books, journals, and digital resources, creating an ideal environment for research and study.

  • Extensive collection of academic resources
  • Digital archive and e-resource access
  • Quiet study spaces and discussion rooms
  • Extended hours during examination periods

State-of-the-Art Laboratories

Our college features modern, well-equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Computer Science.

  • Advanced scientific equipment
  • Computer labs with high-speed internet
  • Specialized research facilities
  • Safety equipment and trained lab assistants

Sports Complex

Our comprehensive sports facilities support both competitive athletics and recreational activities for students.

  • Indoor gymnasium and fitness center
  • Cricket field and football ground
  • Basketball and volleyball courts
  • Swimming pool and athletic track

Modern Auditorium

Our auditorium hosts academic conferences, cultural events, and student performances throughout the year.

  • Seating capacity for 1,000 people
  • State-of-the-art audio-visual system
  • Professional stage and lighting
  • Green rooms and backstage facilities

Student Hostels

Our residential facilities provide a safe, comfortable living environment for students from outside Rangpur.

  • Separate hostels for male and female students
  • 24-hour security and supervision
  • Dining halls with nutritious meal options
  • Study rooms and recreational areas

Admissions

Admission Process

Rangpur Government College welcomes applications from motivated students seeking quality education in a supportive environment.

Our admission process is designed to identify students who will thrive in our academic programs and contribute to our vibrant campus community. We evaluate applications based on academic performance, entrance examination results, and extracurricular achievements.

1

Online Application

Complete the online application form and pay the application fee through our portal.

2

Entrance Examination

Take the required entrance examination for your chosen program of study.

3

Document Verification

Submit original academic certificates and other required documents for verification.

4

Merit List & Admission

Check the merit list and complete the admission process if selected.
