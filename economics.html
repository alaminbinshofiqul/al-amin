About Our Department

Excellence in Economic Education

The Department of Economics at Rangpur Government College stands as a pillar of academic excellence, fostering critical thinking and analytical skills in the field of economics since its establishment.

We provide comprehensive education in microeconomics, macroeconomics, econometrics, and development economics. Our curriculum is designed to prepare students for careers in banking, finance, government, and research.

With modern facilities, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections, we create an environment where students can explore economic theories and their real-world applications.

Key Achievements:

  • 95% graduate employment rate
  • 50+ research publications annually
  • Strong alumni network in banking sector
  • International collaboration programs

Research & Analysis

Our department conducts cutting-edge research in various fields of economics, contributing to policy formulation and economic understanding.

Agricultural Economics

Research on crop pricing, agricultural productivity, and rural market dynamics in northern Bangladesh.

Monetary Policy

Analysis of central bank policies, inflation dynamics, and their impact on economic growth and stability.

Development Economics

Studies on poverty reduction, income inequality, and sustainable development strategies.

International Trade

Research on export-import dynamics, trade policies, and regional economic integration.
