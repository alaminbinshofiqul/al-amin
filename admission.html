Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements include a minimum GPA of 3.5 in previous academic studies, completed application form, and all supporting documents. Some programs may have additional requirements.

Are there any scholarships available?

Yes, Rangpur Government College offers merit-based scholarships, need-based financial aid, and special scholarships for students excelling in sports and cultural activities.

What is the admission process?

The admission process includes online application submission, entrance examination, document verification, and finally, admission confirmation upon selection.

Is hostel accommodation available?

Yes, we have separate hostel facilities for male and female students. Applications for hostel accommodation can be submitted along with the admission form.

How can I contact the admission office?

You can contact our admission office at admission@rgcollege.edu.bd or call at +880-123-456789 during office hours (9 AM to 4 PM, Sunday to Thursday).
