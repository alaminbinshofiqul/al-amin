Our Achievements

Celebrating Excellence and Success

A Legacy of Excellence

For over a century, Rangpur Government College has established itself as a beacon of academic excellence, producing generations of successful graduates who have made significant contributions in various fields both nationally and internationally.

Our institutional achievements span academic excellence, research innovations, cultural accomplishments, and community service, reflecting our commitment to holistic education and societal development.

Academic Excellence

Outstanding Examination Results

Our college has consistently achieved a pass rate of over 90% in national examinations, with numerous students securing positions in the top ten merit lists annually.

Prestigious Scholarships

Over 500 students from Rangpur Government College have received prestigious national and international scholarships including Commonwealth, Fulbright, and various government scholarships.

Higher Education Placements

Our graduates have secured admissions in prestigious universities worldwide, including Oxford, Harvard, MIT, National University of Singapore, and IITs.

Academic Competitions

Students have consistently won national and regional competitions in debates, mathematics olympiads, science fairs, and programming contests.

Research & Innovation

Rangpur Government College has made significant strides in fostering a culture of research and innovation among both faculty and students, with various initiatives yielding remarkable results.

  • Faculty Publications

    Our faculty members have published over 150 research papers in reputed national and international journals across various disciplines.

  • Research Collaborations

    The college has established research partnerships with universities and research institutions both within Bangladesh and internationally.

  • Patent Registrations

    Several innovations developed by our faculty and students have been patented, particularly in the fields of agricultural technology and renewable energy.

  • Student Research Programs

    Our undergraduate research program has enabled students to conduct original research, with many presenting their findings at national conferences.

Cultural & Sports Achievements

National Cultural Competition

Our cultural team has won the National Inter-College Cultural Competition five times in the last decade.

Music and Performing Arts

The college choir and drama team have represented Bangladesh in cultural exchange programs across South Asia.

Literary Publications

Our annual literary magazine "Prothom Alo" has received national recognition for promoting student creativity.

Art Exhibitions

Student artworks have been exhibited in prominent galleries, with several students receiving national art awards.

Cricket Championships

Our cricket team has won the National Inter-College Cricket Tournament three times in the past five years.

Football Excellence

The college football team was the regional champion for four consecutive years (2018-2022).

Athletics Medals

Students have won over 50 medals in various track and field events at the national level in the last decade.

Table Tennis

Our table tennis players have represented Bangladesh in international tournaments, bringing home numerous accolades.
