Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our scholarship programs and application process.

What are the general eligibility requirements for scholarships?
General eligibility requirements include being enrolled as a student at Rangpur Government College, maintaining good academic standing (usually a minimum GPA of 3.0), and meeting specific criteria for each scholarship program such as financial need, merit, or program of study.
How do I apply for multiple scholarships?
You can apply for multiple scholarships through our online portal. Each scholarship has its own application form and requirements. We recommend applying for all scholarships you're eligible for to maximize your chances of receiving financial aid.
When are scholarship applications typically due?
Scholarship deadlines vary throughout the year. Most major scholarships have deadlines between March and August. We recommend checking our scholarship portal regularly and applying early to ensure you don't miss any opportunities.
Can international students apply for scholarships?
Yes, international students are eligible for certain scholarship programs. Some scholarships are specifically designed for international students, while others are open to all students regardless of nationality. Check individual scholarship requirements for specific eligibility criteria.
How are scholarship recipients selected?
Recipients are selected by our scholarship committee based on various factors including academic merit, financial need, leadership potential, community service, and program-specific criteria. The selection process is competitive and fair, ensuring deserving students receive support.
What happens if I receive multiple scholarship awards?
If you receive multiple scholarship awards, our financial aid office will work with you to determine the best combination of awards that maximizes your benefit while adhering to program guidelines. Some scholarships can be combined, while others cannot.
