About Our Department

Excellence in Mathematical Education

The Department of Mathematics at Rangpur Government College stands as a beacon of analytical thinking and mathematical excellence. We nurture logical minds and foster problem-solving skills that extend far beyond the classroom.

Our comprehensive curriculum spans pure mathematics, applied mathematics, statistics, and computational mathematics. We prepare students for careers in academia, research, technology, finance, and data science.

With state-of-the-art computer labs, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections, we provide an environment where mathematical concepts come alive through practical applications.
