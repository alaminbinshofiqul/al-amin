Our Rich History

A legacy of academic excellence spanning over a century

Established in 1902

Rangpur Government College, one of the oldest educational institutions in Bangladesh, was established in 1902 during the British colonial period. What began as a small institution has evolved into a premier center for higher education in northern Bangladesh.

Throughout its illustrious history, the college has maintained its commitment to academic excellence, producing countless scholars, leaders, and professionals who have made significant contributions to society.

The college has witnessed the transformation of the region through various historical periods, standing as a testament to resilience and the pursuit of knowledge through changing times.

Our Journey Through Time

Key milestones in our illustrious history

1902

Foundation

Established as an intermediate college during the British colonial period.

1925

Degree College Status

Upgraded to offer bachelor's degrees in Arts and Sciences.

1947

Post-Partition Era

Continued educational services after the partition of India and Pakistan.

1971

Independence Period

Played a significant role during Bangladesh's independence movement.

1992

Modern Expansion

Introduction of honors and masters programs in multiple disciplines.

2002

Centenary Celebration

Celebrated 100 years of academic excellence and service to the nation.

2022

Digital Transformation

Complete digitization of administrative and educational systems.
